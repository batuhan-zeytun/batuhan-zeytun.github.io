Chess Pieces

  • King
  • Queen
  • Rook
  • Bishop
  • Knight
  • Pawn

The King is the most important piece. Protect it at all costs.

The Queen is the most powerful piece. She moves in all directions.

The Rook moves in straight lines and is great for long attacks.

The Bishop moves diagonally and works well in pairs.

The Knight moves in an L-shape and can jump over other pieces.

Pawns are your front line and can promote when reaching the enemy's back rank.
